Six Paths, One Passion

Welcome to our page — where six bold, curious, and purpose-driven young women come together with one vision: to redefine the future of travel and tourism! We are second-year students taking up Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management at Iloilo Science and Technology University, and this isn’t just our course — it’s our passion, our playground, and our purpose. This website is your front-row pass to who we are, what we dream of, and how we’re making it happen.

From event planning and tourism campaigns to cultural showcases and community engagement, we’re not just learning tourism — we’re living it. Join us as we create, collaborate, and celebrate the art of meaningful travel. Together, we’re not just exploring the world — we’re preparing to lead it.
